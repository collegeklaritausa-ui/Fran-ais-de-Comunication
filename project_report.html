Design Philosophy: L'Élégance Moderne

We adopted a design language that speaks to sophistication and clarity. The palette combines deep midnight blue with champagne gold, evoking a sense of prestige and academic excellence.

  • Typography: Playfair Display (Headings) & Lato (Body)
  • Colors: Midnight Blue (#0A192F) & Champagne Gold (#D4AF37)
  • Layout: Asymmetric, editorial-style with ample whitespace
